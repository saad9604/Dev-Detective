About Us

Welcome to Dev Detective, a website founded by Saad Sayyed, a passionate web developer. Our platform is designed to help developers and GitHub users connect, collaborate, and discover new projects with ease. Saad created Dev Detective with the vision of building a vibrant community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about coding and technology. Our website is the result of his dedication and hard work, and we are proud to offer a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for users to search and explore GitHub profiles and repositories. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, we hope that Dev Detective can be a valuable resource for you. Thank you for visiting our website, and we look forward to helping you navigate the GitHub universe.


At Dev Detective, we are committed to providing a secure and reliable platform for our users. That's why we are constantly working to improve our website and implement new features that will enhance your experience. We also believe in open communication, so if you have any feedback or suggestions for us, please don't hesitate to reach out. We value your input and are always looking for ways to improve. Thank you for being a part of the Dev Detective community!
